📋 Mission Briefing

🎯 Objective

Defend against waves of enemy ships and survive through 50 levels! Defeat bosses every 5 levels to progress!

🎮 Controls

Desktop:

  • Arrow Keys: Move ship
  • Spacebar: Shoot
  • ESC/P: Pause/Resume

Mobile:

  • Touch and drag: Move ship
  • Auto-shoot: Enabled (shoots while touching)
  • Touch anywhere: Shoot continuously

💎 Collectibles

  • 🟢 Health: Restore health
  • 🔵 Shield: Temporary shield
  • 🟡 Weapon: Upgrade weapon
  • 🟣 Missile: Powerful missiles
  • 🟠 Rapid-fire: Faster shooting
  • 🪙 Coins: Small score bonus
  • 💎 Gems: Large score bonus
  • 💰 Money: Used to buy ships
  • 💠 Diamonds: Premium currency

🚀 Ships

  • Gradius: Balanced starter ship
  • Interceptor: Fast and agile
  • Destroyer: Heavy and powerful
  • Battleship: Massive with multiple weapons
  • Stealth: Sleek and dark

🏆 Medals

  • 🥉 Bronze: 5,000+ points
  • 🥈 Silver: 15,000+ points
  • 🥇 Gold: 30,000+ points

🎯 Scoring & Progression

  • Level advancement: Based on enemies destroyed (10 per level)
  • Boss battles: Every 5 levels
  • Combo system: Chain kills for bonus points
  • Streak system: Collect items to build streaks
  • Accuracy tracking: Precision shooting bonus
  • 25 Lives: Extended gameplay

Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 14, 2025

Kaden & Adelynn Adventures ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is (and is not) collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data Collected: The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.
  • Device and Usage Data: The App does not collect device identifiers, usage analytics, crash logs, or any other technical data.
  • Game Data: Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device or in your iCloud account if enabled. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or SDKs. No data is shared with external services.

3. Children’s Privacy

This App is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete such information.

4. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no information to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about your privacy or data, please contact us using the method below.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub Issues page.

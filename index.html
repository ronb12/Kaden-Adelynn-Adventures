🎮 Game Features

🚀 4 Unique Ships

Choose from Fighter, Interceptor, Blaster, or Cruiser. Each ship has different speed, fire rate, and damage characteristics.

⚔️ 4 Weapon Types

Laser, Plasma, Missile, and Spread weapons. Each has unique damage, speed, and firing patterns.

⭐ Power-Up System

Collect health, weapon, speed, and shield power-ups during gameplay for temporary boosts and upgrades.

💰 Collectibles

Gather golden collectibles for bonus points. Different enemies give different point values.

🎯 Progressive Difficulty

Enemies become more challenging as you level up. Score more points to advance to higher levels.

🏆 High Score System

Your best score is automatically saved. Try to beat your record with different ship and weapon combinations!

🎮 Controls

  • Arrow Keys or WASD - Move ship
  • Spacebar - Shoot
  • Mobile: Touch and drag to move ship
  • Mobile: Touch anywhere to auto-shoot
  • Collect power-ups for upgrades
  • Gather golden collectibles for points
  • Avoid letting enemies pass you

🎯 Game Tips

Beginner: Start with Fighter + Laser for balanced gameplay. Focus on avoiding enemies and collecting power-ups.

Advanced: Try Cruiser + Missile for maximum damage, or Interceptor + Spread for rapid-fire coverage.

Pro: Master the timing of power-up collection and weapon switching for maximum efficiency.

🌟 Power-Up Guide

❤️ Health: Restores 1 life (maximum 5 lives)

⚔️ Weapon: Cycles through weapon types - Laser → Plasma → Missile → Spread

⚡ Speed: Temporary speed boost for 5 seconds

🛡️ Shield: Temporary invincibility for 5 seconds
